How to make a Plane



Step 1: Prepare a Rectangular piece of Paper of your choice of Colour and Size



Step 2: Fold the Two Corners from the Shorter side of the Rectangle so that they create an Isosceles Triangle



Step 3: Flip the Paper to the Other Side



Step 4: Fold the Isosceles Triangle down to the Front



Step 5: Fold the Upper Corners to the Center Line of the Triangle, but leave a Small Space on the Upper Side



Step 6: Fold the Center Corner of the Triangle to Secure the Previous Folds



Step 7: Fold the Shape in Half through the Backside



Step 8: Fold the Side with the Obtuse Angle Inwards so as it lines up with the Other Side



Step 9: Repeat Step 3: Flip the Paper to the Other Side



Step 10: Repeat Step 8: Fold the Side with the Obtuse Angle Inwards so as it lines up with the Other Side



Step 11: Spread the Wings and Enjoy your Product
