How to make a Ship



Step 1: Prepare a Square piece of Paper of your choice in Colour and Size



Step 2: Fold the Four Corners into the Center of the Square



Step 3: Flip the Paper to the Other Side



Step 4: Repeat the 2nd Step: Fold the Four Corners into the Center of the Square



Step 5: Repeat the 3rd Step: Flip the Paper to the Other Side



Step 6: Repeat the 2nd Step: Fold the Four Corners into the Center of the Square



Step 7: Repeat the 3rd Step: Flip the Paper to the Other Side



Step 8: Open two Facing Slits and Pinch them into a Rectangular Shape



Step 9: Pull the Remaining Slits so that the previous ones stand up next to each other



Step 10: Enjoy your Final Product
