How to make a Lily



Step 1: Prepare a Square piece of Paper of your choice in Colour and Size



Step 2: Fold the Paper in Half into an Isosceles Triangle



Step 3: Open a Side of the Triangle and Fold it into a Small Square



Step 4: Flip the Paper to the Other Side



Step 5: Repeat Step 3: Open the Remaining Side of the Triangle and Fold it into a Small Square



Step 6: Open the Left Piece of the Square and Press it Down similarly to Step 3 to create a Diamond



Step 7: Open the Left and Right Corners of the Diamond and Press it Down similarly to Step 3 and create a Triangle



Step 8: Fold the newly formed Triangle down



Step 9: Fold Half of the Whole Diamond to the Right



Step 10: Fold the Half Piece of the Square to the Back



Step 11: Repeat for the next Three sides



Step 12: Flip each Layer and find a Whole Diamond without Overlaps



Step 13: Fold the Left and Right Corners of the Diamond to the Center so as the Two Sides overlap the Center Line



Step 14: Fold the Upper Layer downwards



Step 15: Repeat the same process for the next Three Sides



Step 16: Release the Layers that were folded downwards, curl them, and Enjoy your Product
