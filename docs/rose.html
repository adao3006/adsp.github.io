How to make a Rose



Step 1: Prepare a Square piece of Paper of your choice in Colour and Size



Step 2: Fold the Paper Horizontally and Vertically, then Unfold to create Fold Marks



Step 3: Fold the Paper Diagonally, then Unfold to create Fold Marks



Step 4: Fold the Horizontal Marks into the Center, compressing the Paper into a Triangle



Step 5: Bring the Two Tips of one Triangle with the same angle to the Tip with the bigger angle, folding the paper



Step 6: Open a Side of the Triangles and Fold them into Small Squares



Step 7: Flip the Paper to the Other Side



Step 8: Repeat Step 5: Bring the Two Tips of one Triangle with the same angle to the Tip with the bigger angle, folding the paper



Step 9: Repeat Step 6: Open a Side of the Triangles and Fold them into Small Squares



Step 10: Fold the Triangle downwards so that the big tip touches the side of the Rectangle



Step 11: Rotate the Paper 180 degrees



Step 12: Open that Other Side to find a Myriad of Shapes



Step 13: Fold the Two Zig-Zag Sides into Triangles, Flattening the Paper in the process



Step 14: Repeat Step 7: Flip the Paper to the Other Side



Step 15: Make the Triangle stand straight up



Step 16: Grab a Thin piece of the Triangle and Fold Half of it down into one of the Squares



Step 17: Rotate the Paper to the Other Side of the Triangle



Step 18: Repeat Step 16 Grab a Thin piece of the Triangle and Fold Half of it down into one of the Squares



Step 19: Grab the Center of the Four Standing Triangles and Twist it tightly while holding the bottom of the Paper with your palm



Step 20: Curl the Outer Petals and Enjoy your Product
