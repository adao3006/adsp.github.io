How to make a Crane



Step 1: Prepare a Square piece of Paper of your choice in Colour and Size



Step 2: Fold the Paper in Half into an Isosceles Triangle



Step 3: Open a Side of the Triangle and Fold it into a Small Square



Step 4: Flip the Paper to the Other Side



Step 5: Repeat Step 3: Open the Remaining Side of the Triangle and Fold it into a Small Square



Step 6: Open a Corner of the Square and Fold it into a Diamond shape



Step 7: Repeat Step 4: Flip the Paper to the Other Side



Step 8: Repeat Step 6: Open the Remaining Corner of the Square and Fold it into a Diamond shape



Step 9: Fold Two Sides of the Diamond so as it overlaps the Line at the Center of the Diamond



Step 10: Repeat Step 4: Flip the Paper to the Other Side



Step 11: Repeat Step 9: Fold Two Sides of the Diamond so as it overlaps the Line at the Center of the Diamond



Step 12: Open the Slits on the Sides of the New Shape and Fold them into Two Spikes



Step 13: Pinch the Tip of one of the Spikes to create a Beak



Step 14: Spread the Wings and Enjoy your Product
